Loki(2021)

Loki is a television series from Marvel Studios, streaming on Disney+, centered around the character Loki, played by Tom Hiddleston. The series takes place after the events of "Avengers: Endgame" (2019), where an alternate version of Loki escapes with the Tesseract.

Avatar: The Way of Water(2022)

"Avatar: The Way of Water" is the sequel to James Cameron's 2009 film "Avatar." Released in 2022, this movie continues the story of Jake Sully and Neytiri as they navigate new challenges in the world of Pandora. The sequel delves deeper into Pandora's ecosystem, particularly its aquatic environments, showcasing the Na'vi's connection to the water and introducing new characters and creatures. The film is known for its groundbreaking visual effects, much like its predecessor, and its exploration of themes related to nature, family, and survival.

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba(2019)

A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive - Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko, who is turning into a demon slowly. Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister.

Man of Steel(2013)

An alien child is evacuated from his dying world and sent to Earth to live among humans. His peace is threatened when other survivors of his home planet invade Earth.

Baahubali: The Beginning(2015)

The story is set in the ancient kingdom of Mahishmati. It revolves around the rivalry between two cousins, Baahubali and Bhallaladeva, and their quest to ascend the throne of Mahishmati. The film follows the journey of Shivudu, a young man who discovers his true identity as Mahendra Baahubali, the son of the slain king Amarendra Baahubali.
